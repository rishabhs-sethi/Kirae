Book Recommendation


Top 5 motivational books and Why should you read them !



Sometimes going through life can be very difficult and demotivating. You have to face ups and downs and most of the time downs but you just have to make sure that you don't give up.


Reading motivational books can really change our perspective in seeing ourselves and the world around us. A habit of reading a good motivational book as part of the morning routine can be really life-transforming.


“We need to accept that we won’t always make the right decisions, that we’ll screw up royally sometimes – understanding that failure is not the opposite of success, it’s part of success.”


Here are some of the books that will help you to stay motivated and help you walk through difficult time. These books will inspire you and will challenge you to question your current perspective and grow in an unexpected way.


1. THE BIG PICTURE: A GUIDE TO FIND YOUR PURPOSE IN LIFE

Author: Christine B. Whelan



"Every problem has a solution you just have to find it"


What am I going to do in my life? Its a question that crosses everyone mind once in there life. Finding answer to this question can be very difficult and demotivating, but once you read 'the big picture' i am not saying that you will surely find the answer but i guarantee it will be less demotivating and frustrating.

The Big Picture is a guide to figuring out what matters and how to make it happen. It helps readers to harness their talents, interests, and values to take action, address problems, create solutions, and touch the lives of others.



2. HOW TO STOP WORRYING AND START LIVING

Author: Dale Carnegie



“Our thoughts make us what we are.”


Most of the time we waste worrying about different things like what will be our future if we take this step now or will i ever be good enough for that person or other things. How to Stop Worrying and Start Living is a self-help book. This book simply teaches us how to live our life to the fullest and stop overthinking ,it also make people more aware of , not only themselves but other people around them. If you are the overthinking type of person stop worrying and start reading.

How to stop worrying and start living teaches us why worrying is bad for you and what you can do about it. Carnegie tells many key points to stop worrying in this book and some of them are : a) Live for today: Keep the worries of yesterday and tomorrow out of your head. b) Make decision:Once you've decided how to solve your worries, take action and don't look back. c) Be happier and focus on positive things and act everything is good. d) Be yourself: There is no one like you nor will there ever be on this earth. e) Analyze:Accept and improve on the worst case scenario.



3. SCREW IT LETS DO IT: LESSONS IN LIFE

Author: Richard Branson




"Most of the important things in the world have been accomplished by people who kept on trying when there seemed to be no hope at all"


Screw it lets do it its title justify the book perfectly. When you start something new there will be many people to pull you down or make fun of your work ,there will always be people to demotivate you but you just have to ignore them , just do it believe whatever you want to achieve can be achieved. Change the world even in a small way, because we all know actions speak louder than words.

Screw it let's do it is about Richard Branson own journey as he started his business as tiny record store,called virgin records and now he is the head of one of worlds most recognized and respected groups of companies. He also share many business tips and what are the problems faced by him but he never gave up.This Book also presents Branson's vision of future. Screw it let's do it is revolutionary, approach to business,engaging, funny utterly absorbing and above all Inspiring.



4. THE ART OF HAPPINESS: A HANDBOOK FOR LIVING

Author: Dalai Lama




"Start doing things that makes you happy no matter what others thinks about it"


Have you ever thought what is the main purpose of your life. You have earn a lot of money, achieved your goal etc and still you are not happy with your life. according to Dalai lama the main purpose of your life should be to achieve happiness. It is said that "not all rich people are happy but all happy people are rich",happiness is determined more by the state of one's mind than by one's external conditions.

The Art of happiness is how Dalai lama achieved inner peace, calmness, and happiness. He talked about 3 main key lessons in his book for seeking happiness : a) You don't have to be religious, to be spiritual: It doesn't matter which religion you belong to, or whether you belong to any for that matter. you should be spiritual, being compassionate, a good person, caring for one another is more important than following any religion. b) The only constant thing is change:Suffering is a part of life, but by rejecting that we make ourselves into victims and start blaming.The only constant is change. You can cry for hours after a loved one dies but the moment you accept the change you will be on the path of happiness. c) Know your limits: Always be honest with yourself and others about what you can and cannot do. Being ok with your limits requires self awareness which is very important. And if you don't understand something try this, say"I don't understand" it will show how strong you are.



5. DON'T SWEAT THE SMALL STUFF

Author: Richard Branson




"Always look towards positive side of the problem it will become less hard to handle"


It's the little things that drive us insane day after day for example traffic congestion, awful service at a restaurant, someone taking credit for your work in office etc. This creates stress which makes the little problem to deal with. This book will help you to find happiness in this busy and hectic world. Carlson believes that we can learn to look at life from a different perspective, which will melt stress and bring new joy to life.

This book contains 100 short chapters of maximum 4-5 pages. Each chapter tells you how to live a stress free and graceful life. Some of the chapters are : a) Make peace with imperfection b) Think of your problems as potential teachers c) Do one thing at a time Carlson always believed that when we achieve "wise person in me" perspective, our problems will be more controllable and life more peaceful.


I HOPE I WAS ABLE TO HELP YOU AND REMEMBER YOU ARE BORN TO BE A WINNER AND DON'T EVER GIVE UP, YOU ARE NOT ALONE.

HAPPY READING, STAY SAFE AND STAY MOTIVATED
