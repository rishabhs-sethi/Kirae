Tips and Tricks


Techniques to Improve Concentration while reading !



Nowadays its a quite difficult task to concentrate during reading. Everyone has faced this problem at least once in their life. So is there any solution? Of course as we all know “every problem has a solution” but before I tell you some techniques and tips to improve your concentration lets know what are the reasons for bad concentration.


Reasons that effect our concentration

In this distracting world it is quite difficult to read books with concentration but it’s not impossible. Being a Student or Professional, It might be quite difficult to concentrate on reading properly. Social media, friends, family, are some of the reasons. If you are chatting with someone for 5 minutes, you will never know how the time pased.


"KEEP READING IT'S ONE OF THE MOST MARVELOUS ADVENTURES THAT ANYONE CAN HAVE"


Everyone can relate to this situation. So we will discuss about some research-based techniques that will helps you to get out of this situation :

1. USE YOUR HAND TO GUIDE YOUR EYES WHILE READING



“A reader lives a thousand lives before he dies . . . The man who never reads lives only one.”


'It's a fact that our eyes are naturally attracted to motion'. So if you move your finger eyes will follow and you can concentrate on reading. If you feel uncomfortable with hand try using a pen or pencil.

Move your hand from left to right smoothly and make sure that your speed is neither too fast nor too slow or it will effect your reading.


2. MAKE READING CREATIVE



“Reading is essential for those who seek to rise above the ordinary.”


It is the most amazing and effective Technique.The most common problem faced by almost everyone is when you open the book all you see is written text which sometimes seems very boring.

So here is the simple solution to your problem, make a book mark related to your favorite animal or anything you like, which will keep you engaged. For example i like mouse so my bookmark is in that shape. Some other creative techniques are : 1) Start visualizing the scenes in your mind. 2) Make creative cartoons or sketches on the book's corners . 3) Underline with different pens.


3. SET A TARGET



“Think before you speak. Read before you think.”


When you start reading a book always remember to set a target like Read 10 pages or complete number of chapters as per your convenience. It motivates you to achieve your target and improve your concentration. Do competition with yourself.

Add reading to your daily life routine, which will change your perspective towards life and help you grow your mindset in a unique way.


4. PEACEFUL PLACE TO READ



“People can lose their lives in libraries. They ought to be warned.”


Find a place in your house, where no one disturbs you. Make sure the place is neat and clean for proper focus. You can also decorate the place with relaxing and dim lights and if possible put some beautiful flowers/plants. Keep something to drink near you or a bottle of water.

Most of the people says that when you read keep your phone away from you but i don't think its a good idea it will distract you even more because you will keep thinking about your calls and messages,so what you can do is keep your phone near you and switch off your Wi-Fi. This will keep you focused.


5. LISTEN PEACEFUL MUSIC



“Once you have read a book you care about, some part of it is always with you.”


As we all know that listening music is the best medicine for our brain. Personally this is my favorite technique, everyone like listening music and it also reduce anxiety and pain as well as improve mood and mental alertness.

When you sit to read play music without lyrics and high beat, listen simple slow music like classical songs or meditation music as it is meant to relieve stress and relax your mind and improve your concentration.


I HOPE I WAS ABLE TO HELP YOU AND DON'T FORGET TO TRY THESE TECHNIQUES FOR 21 DAYS YOU WILL SEE THE RESULT YOURSELF.

HAPPY READING, STAY SAFE AND STAY FOCUSED
