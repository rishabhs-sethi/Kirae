ABOUT US

Kirae is an online platform for booklovers and book enthusiasts. We want to ensure happy and carefree reading for people. We believe in the notion of being “vocal in local” and hence, we directly connect local vendors with consumers from the comfort of home. Readers get to enjoy easy access to diverse range of books through sanitized contactless home deliveries. We are into opening multiple access options- rent, purchase, or donate books- for our clients. We aim to encourage and motivate generous donors through certificates.
